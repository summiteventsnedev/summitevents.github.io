Summit Events New England is New England's most premier event management company servicing a rich & diverse atmosphere of personalized functions.

Our Story:

In early 2019 Summit Events was founded with the idea of creating events & experiences curated to last a lifetime. We offer an all-inclusive experience tailored to your exact needs and desires and offer a staff of seasoned professionals at your disposal.

In our world, the little things matter & personalization is paramount. Your special day needs an attentive coordinator so that you can enjoy the atmosphere of friends and family. We all know the persistence & attention that goes into planning an event; with Summit on your side the heavy lifting is done. Whether it be a family, friend or corporate function; Summit Events will organize and manage your event from beginning to end. We embody the idea that with great attention to detail and a staff with years of experience, we can create monumental moments.

Summit will bring your vision to life in the most spectacular of ways and we specialize in detail and elegance. Your event will be assigned a specific coordinator within our team, ready to listen and learn your expectations from inception to execution. Summit Events is your one stop on the road to memories that will last a lifetime.


Meet The Team

Christopher M. Genco – Owner/Director of Operations

Christopher Genco is the owner & Director of Operations for Summit Events New England LLC. In early 2019, Christopher set out with the idea that excellence is achievable through attention to detail, personalization and proper attitude. Christopher’s scope of Summit Events included all aspects of conception, consultation and execution. Graduating from Johnson & Wales University, Christopher holds degrees in Culinary Arts, Pastry Arts, Restaurant Management and Organizational Leadership. Under his leadership, Summit Events has taken off with vigor & poise.

Christopher has worked in the hospitality industry for over 15 years, in both culinary and management sectors. Some of the more notable companies include Ritz Carlton, Marriott Hotels & Compass Group. Christopher’s years of experience allow him to understand the client’s dreams and his history of leadership enables him to prime the Summit team to make those dreams a reality. Christopher’s outgoing & professional demeanor allow him to connect strongly with Summit’s many clients.
